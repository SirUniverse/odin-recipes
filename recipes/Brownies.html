Cool Brownies

Recipe

Lets start with the ingredients:

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter(Melted)
  • 1/3 cocoa powder
  • pinch of salt

    • Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8x8- or 9x9-inch baking pan.
    2. Beat together sugar and butter in a medium bowl. Add eggs and mix well. Combine flour, cocoa, and salt; stir into sugar mixture.
    3. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are firm, 25 to 30 minutes. Cool before cutting.
